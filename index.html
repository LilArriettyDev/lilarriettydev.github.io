Get to Know Me

Hi there! I'm a fun-loving first-grader at Monipur High School & College in Dhaka. I'm wild about robots and believe they're not just cool—they're the future, and I want to be part of it.
But, there's more to me than just robotics! I'm a fan of exploring new places, practicing martial arts, and getting creative with crafts—quilling is my absolute favorite. I love the magic of turning simple paper into beautiful art.
I have big dreams. One day, I hope the robots I design will make a difference in people's lives around the world.
Feel free to explore my website and check out my projects. Thanks for stopping by!
